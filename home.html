Why I love My college Fests ?

Regalia

The RCC Institute of Information Technology is organising their annual cultural fest REGALIA, to be held at Sarat Sadan, Howrah on 27 & 28 April 2023. REGALIA is the most notable among the plethora of fests hosted by RCCIIT throughout the year and this year’s extravaganza is powered by Novacle Technologies in association with Alumni Association of RCCIIT.....

Alphatec

The annual Departmental Technical Fest Alphatec (conducted over 2 days by the Student Welfare Committee) and explores the technical acumen, intelligence and natural talents of the students through Codathon (Coding & Debugging)/ Gaming, Mathemagic, Spell-Bee, Su+doku, Quiz competitions, model making, robotics etc......

8 May 2023

Techtrix

Techtrix is the Annual Technical Fest of RCC Institute of Information Technology, Kolkata. It was a 3 day event from 10th to 12th April 2023 which was held in the college campus. There were events in several categories like Robotics, Gamings, Competitive Coding, Flagships and Non-Technical fun stuff along with the Smart Bengal Hackathon.

8 Feb 2023

Games of Thrones

Game Of Thrones (GOT'24), RCC Institute of Information Technology's Annual Sports Meet, is back as a National Inter College Sports Tournament in Kolkata happening in January 2024. Featuring diverse sports competitions and attractive prizes, GoT'24 invites colleges nationwide for intense and inclusive contests. Beyond rivalry, it emphasizes sportsmanship and teamwork, offering a platform for students to showcase talents and create lasting memories. Don't miss the biggest sports event of the year – mark your calendars!
