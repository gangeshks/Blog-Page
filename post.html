Art, Culture and Sports

Written by RCCIIT

Monday Jan 22

The college life becomes more enjoyable to the students when, besides hardcore professional studies, they enjoy the freedom to nurture their hobbies or talents in creative & performing arts like singing, dancing, painting, acting (drama), playing musical instruments, photography, story/poem/literary article writing (in magazine) and so on apart from taking part in games & sports, quiz, debate and other techno-fun competitions. The students showcase their multi-faceted talents through the annual fests/regular club activities and cultural magazines (like Verve and Kalmadhumas) or technical magazines (like Power Story and MCA Tech Mag)

The Student Welfare Committee organizes annual cultural festival REGALIAX comprising of number of performing arts like solo vocal, band, dance, drama, choreography, fashion show etc. Students of other colleges in and around the city take part in this 2 or 3-day long annual extravaganza full of colour and vibes. The performance by popular national or state level artists or bands adds another dimension to the fest.

The annual games and sports festival Game of Thrones (GOT) is also organized by the Student Welfare Committee and it includes competitions of both indoor and outdoor games including Badminton, Carom, Chess, Table Tennis, Volley Ball, Football, Cricket , Tug of War.

The annual Technical Fest TECHTRIX (conducted over 2 days by the Student Welfare Committee) and departmental technical programs like Innovision or Alphatec explores the technical acumen, intelligence and natural talents of the students through Codathon (Coding & Debugging)/ Gaming, Mathemagic, Spell-Bee, Su+doku, Quiz competitions, model making, robotics etc.

The institute traditionally encourages, promotes and sponsors such student fests and extra-curricular activities so as to help unleash the creative and natural talents of the students.
